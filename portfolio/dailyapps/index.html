DailyApps by XEFI

DailyApps by XEFI is an all-in-one digital solution that simplifies HR and administrative management for TPEs and PMEs.

Associated with XEFI Center Mauritius, the platform brings together the essential tools businesses need to manage day-to-day internal workflows from a single, secure, and intuitive environment.

With the Essentio offer, companies can manage:
  • Leave requests and absences
  • Expense reports
  • Surveys and questionnaires
  • Onboarding and employee guides
  • Administrative processes in one place

DailyApps is fully hosted in France and works alongside existing HR tools, helping small and medium-sized businesses save time, automate repetitive tasks, and improve team productivity.

Backed by 28 years of expertise, 147 local agencies, and more than 25,000 clients, DailyApps makes digital transformation more accessible for growing businesses.
