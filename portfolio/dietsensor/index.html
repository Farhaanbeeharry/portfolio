DietSensor

DietSensor is a revolutionary nutrition coaching app designed to help users take control of their health through science-backed, personalized nutrition programs.

Associated with DietSensor Inc., the app was created by Astrid and Remy Bonnasse, whose family story inspired its mission. DietSensor empowers individuals to make better dietary choices for lasting wellness and disease prevention.

With more than 37 fully customized programs, DietSensor provides tailored guidance for goals such as:
  • Weight loss
  • Muscle gain
  • Intermittent fasting
  • Ketogenic diets
  • Diabetes management
  • Cardiovascular health

Users can access 36 weekly meal plans, 600 healthy recipes, and smart shopping lists, all adapted to their preferences, health profiles, and goals.

The app also helps users analyze meals through voice input or barcode scanning, identify unbalanced eating habits, and receive real-time nutritional insights powered by a large food database. Integration with Apple Health and Google Fit supports seamless activity tracking and hydration monitoring.

Named a CES Best of Innovation Award Honoree, DietSensor combines technology, science, and compassion to make expert nutrition guidance more accessible.

Website: https://dietsensor.com/

Download the app on the App Store or Google Play to start building smarter, healthier eating habits.
