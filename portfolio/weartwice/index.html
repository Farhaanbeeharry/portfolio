WearTwice

WearTwice is a mobile app that transforms how people in the UAE buy and sell second-hand fashion. Designed for simplicity, speed, and sustainability, it helps users clear out their wardrobes, earn money, and shop more consciously in one trusted platform.

Associated with Weartwice.com, the app creates a smooth experience for both sellers and buyers.

For sellers, WearTwice offers:
  • A zero-commission selling experience
  • Quick listing by snapping photos of items
  • Easy price setting and instant publishing

For buyers, the app provides:
  • Access to thousands of pre-loved fashion items
  • Direct negotiation with sellers
  • Fast and secure delivery within 24 hours

Backed by secure payments through MAMO and a strong focus on trust and safety, WearTwice makes sustainable fashion more accessible to everyone.

WearTwice invites users to join the fashion revolution in the UAE by downloading the app on the App Store or Google Play and experiencing a smarter way to shop, sell, and save sustainably.
